About Us

The name Gradxtech is curved from the vision to empower young professionals and graduate in Tech i.e GRADXTECH.

We are on a mission to bridge the gap between young graduates, NYSC members, and the global tech industry. Founded with the vision of democratizing access to tech education, we provide structured training programs designed to turn absolute beginners into industry-ready professionals.

Our curriculum is designed to equip you with the skills you need to thrive in the digital world. With goal to equip the next generation with the skills, confidence, and opportunities they need to build successful tech careers. We believe that anyone, regardless of their background, can excel in technology with the right training and support.
